Approach

I believe in starting with human behavior before building anything. Most failed products and ineffective training programs ignore how people actually think and act.

For products, I prototype fast with AI tools, build lean with proven technologies, and optimize for genuine human connection over engagement metrics.

For training, I focus on practical application over theory. Every workshop includes tools participants can use immediately in their work. My background in interpersonal communication, negotiation strategy, and conflict management helps me facilitate productive group dynamics and stakeholder alignment.
